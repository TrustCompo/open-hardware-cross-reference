Open Hardware Cross Reference

Useful replacement references for hardware engineers

This GitHub Pages site publishes practical lifecycle and replacement pages for parts affected by obsolescence, NRND status, or supply-chain risk. Each page is designed to stand on its own before linking back to the matching TrustCompo product page.

Visit TrustCompo

What you will find

Lifecycle status, evidence summaries, replacement buckets, and quick comparison context.

Why it exists

To help engineers review alternatives faster while still validating footprint, electrical limits, and qualification details.

Current state

Repository scaffold is ready. Published part pages and vendor indexes will appear here as automation runs.

Planned public structure

Pages are generated into site/parts/, vendor browse pages into site/vendors/, and machine-readable exports into site/data/.
