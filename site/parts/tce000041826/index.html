Functional higher-current alternative to the obsolete onsemi NCP1117ST25T3G. Microchip MIC29150-12WU is a 1.5 A high-current LDO (50% more current than NCP1117's 1.0 A) in TO-263-3 (3-pin D2PAK). NOT pin-compatible (TO-263 vs SOT-223) — PCB redesign required. Same low-dropout topology and 3-terminal LDO architecture. Use when the application needs >1.0 A and can accommodate the larger TO-263 thermal footprint. Note: 12 V fixed output; for 2.5 V fixed order MIC29150-2.5WU.

Target product summary

TCE000041826 · NCP1117ST25T3G

1.0 A Low-Dropout Positive Fixed and Adjustable Voltage Regulators<br/>The NCP1117 series are low dropout positive voltage regulators that are capable of providing an output current that is in excess of 1.0 A with a maximum dropout voltage of 1.2 V at 800 mA over temperature. This series contains eight fixed output voltages of 1.5 V, 1.8 V, 2.0 V, 2.5 V, 2.85 V, 3.3 V, 5.0 V, and 12 V that have no minimum load requirement to maintain regulation. Also included is an adjustable output version that can be programmed from 1.25 V to 18.8 V with two external resistors. On chip trimming adjusts the reference/output voltage to within ±1.0% accuracy. Internal protection features consist of output current limiting, safe operating area compensation, and thermal shutdown. <br/>The NCP1117 series can operate with up to 20 V input. Devices are available in SOT−223 and DPAK packages.<br/>Features<br/>• Output Current in Excess of 1.0 A<br/>• 1.2 V Maximum Dropout Voltage at 800 mA Over Temperature<br/>• Fixed Output Voltages of 1.5 V, 1.8 V, 2.0 V, 2.5 V, 2.85 V, 3.3 V, 5.0 V, and 12 V<br/>• Adjustable Output Voltage Option<br/>• No Minimum Load Requirement for Fixed Voltage Output Devices<br/>• Reference/Output Voltage Trimmed to ±1.0%<br/>• Current Limit, Safe Operating and <br/>Thermal Shutdown Protection<br/>• Operation to 20 V Input<br/>• NCV Prefix for Automotive and Other Applications Requiring Site and Control Changes<br/>• Pb−Free Packages are AvailableApplications<br/>• Consumer and Industrial Equipment Point of Regulation<br/>• Active SCSI Termination for 2.85 V Version<br/>• Switching Power Supply Post Regulation<br/>• Hard Drive Controllers<br/>• Battery Chargers
