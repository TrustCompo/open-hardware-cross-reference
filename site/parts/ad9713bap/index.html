Target product summary

AD9713BAP

ADI currently lists the AD9713B family as obsolete and advises designers to consider AD975X or AD976X successors. We published AD9713BAP as the complete MPN for this obsolete 12-bit 80 MSPS TTL-compatible DAC variant, and sustainment programs should recheck logic compatibility, output architecture, timing margins, package fit, and dynamic performance before approving AD9760ARZ as the practical replacement candidate.
