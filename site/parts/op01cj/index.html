Target product summary

OP01CJ

ADI marks the OP01 family as obsolete and suggests OP177 as the replacement path. We published OP01CJ as the complete MPN for this obsolete precision operational amplifier variant, and any sustainment substitution should recheck offset, gain-bandwidth, stability, supply rails, and package constraints before approving OP177GSZ in a legacy analog design.
