Target product summary

AD9702BW

ADI marks the AD9702 family as obsolete and points users to ADV7123 as the suggested replacement. We corrected the package presentation for AD9702BW to the legacy 24-CDIP form reflected by distributor references, and any migration to ADV7123JSTZ330 should include a deliberate review of channel count, speed, output behavior, package constraints, video timing, and downstream analog interface assumptions before release.
