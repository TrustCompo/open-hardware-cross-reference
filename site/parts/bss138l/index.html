Open Hardware Cross Reference

BSS138L

TrustCompo code: TCE000056780

Functional alternative to the obsolete onsemi BSS138L. Vishay Si2302CDS-T1-E3 is a 20 V N-channel MOSFET in SOT-23-3 with identical pinout (1=Gate, 2=Drain, 3=Source) to BSS138L. Much lower RDS(on) (0.045 Ω at Vgs = 4.5 V) and higher ID (2.6 A) than BSS138L. ONLY use as functional drop-in when the application stays below 20 V VDS (e.g. 3.3 V or 5 V rail-level shifting). NOT a substitute in 24 V or 48 V circuits. Verify VDS margin before substitution.

Target product summary

BSS138L · TCE000056780

BSS138L is onsemi's SOT-23-3 N-channel logic-level enhancement mode MOSFET rated 50 V VDS, 200 mA ID, 3.5 Ω Rds(on) at Vgs = 4.5 V, 350 mW Pd. onsemi product page (2025) shows lifecycle status 'Last Shipments' — the onsemi lifecycle glossary defines 'Last Shipments' as a 6-month period of final shipments, after which the part becomes Obsolete. DigiKey lists Part Status as 'Obsolete' for BSS138L. Direct P2P drop-in: Diodes Incorporated BSS138-7-F (SOT-23-3, 50 V, 200 mA, 3.5 Ω Rds(on)) — identical pinout and pin-for-pin spec match.

Series: BSS138 | Datasheet: https://datasheet.trustcompo.com/on-semiconductor-bss138l.pdf
