Target product summary

AD705JR

ADI marks the AD705 family as obsolete and recommends OP97 as the suggested replacement. We published AD705JR as the complete MPN for this obsolete low-bias-current bipolar op amp variant, and any migration to OP97FSZ should include a fresh review of bias current, offset, bandwidth, supply range, package fit, and long-term precision behavior in the target circuit.
