TCE000056779

Functional low-power alternative to the obsolete onsemi NC7SZ125. 74AUP1G125GW is the Nexperia AUP (Advanced Ultra-low Power) single bus buffer gate in SOT-23-5 with identical pinout to NC7SZ125. Operates 0.8 V to 3.6 V VCC, <1 uA quiescent current, ~0.9 ns propagation delay. Same 3-state buffer function, lower power envelope than NC7SZ's UHS family. Use as drop-in for battery-powered and low-leakage applications where NC7SZ's UHS current is excessive.

Target product summary

TCE000056779 · NC7SZ125

NC7SZ125 is onsemi's single TinyLogic UHS buffer / line driver with three-state output in SOT-23-5. Fabricated in advanced CMOS, propagation delay ~2.0 ns at 5 V, quiescent current <1 µA. onsemi has marked this family 'DISCONTINUED: These devices are not available' in the official datasheet Rev. 4 (Page 5). For new designs use SN74LVC1G125DBVR (TI) which is the industry-standard pin-compatible drop-in.

SN74AHC1G125DBVR

TCE000056784 · Functional · Fit score 60

SN74AHC1G125DBVR is TI's SOT-23-5 single bus buffer gate with 3-state output in the AHC (Advanced High-Speed CMOS) family. Operating voltage 2 V to 5.5 V, ±8 mA output drive, ~3.5 ns propagation delay at 5 V. Pin-for-pin compatible with onsemi NC7SZ125 (SOT-23-5, same pinout). AHC is the legacy higher-drive equivalent of LVC; same 3-state buffer function. Suitable as functional alternative where higher drive current is required (vs the lower-power UHS NC7SZ family).

SN74AUP1G125DBVR

TCE000056783 · P2P · Fit score 80

SN74AUP1G125DBVR is TI's SOT-23-5 single low-power bus buffer gate with 3-state output. AUP family supports 0.8 V to 3.6 V VCC, ~0.9 ns propagation delay at 3.3 V, <1 uA quiescent current. Pin-for-pin drop-in for onsemi NC7SZ125 (SOT-23-5, same pinout 1=OE, 2=A, 3=GND, 4=Y, 5=VCC). Listed in TI's Little Logic Guide as the industry cross-reference for the NC7SZ family. Lower power and lower voltage than NC7SZ; compatible at the 1.8 V to 3.3 V operating range where NC7SZ is typically deployed.

SN74LVC1G125DBVR

TCE000056781 · P2P · Fit score 100

SN74LVC1G125DBVR is TI's SOT-23-5 single bus buffer gate with 3-state output (active-low OE). LVC family supports 1.65 V to 5.5 V VCC, ±24 mA output drive, 3.7 ns propagation delay at 3.3 V. Pin-for-pin drop-in for onsemi NC7SZ125 (SOT-23-5 footprint, identical pinout: 1=OE, 2=A, 3=GND, 4=Y, 5=VCC). Industry-standard cross-reference listed on Z2Data and Ovaga for the obsolete onsemi NC7SZ125. Same CMOS topology, same 3-state output, same quiescent current class.
