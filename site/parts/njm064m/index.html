Target product summary

NJM064M · TCE000041557

NJM064M is a legacy DMP-14 / DIP-14 compatible member of Nisshinbo Micro Devices' NJM064 low-power quad J-FET input operational amplifier family. As of June 21, 2026, the current official Nisshinbo public references we verified are the NJM064C/NJM064CA pages, which describe the NJM064 family as the low-power version of the NJM072C/074C family and show 1 MHz typ GBW, 3.5 V/us typ slew rate, and 4 V to 36 V supply range. We did not verify a public manufacturer EOL/PDN notice for the exact NJM064M package variant, so this catalog entry should not present NJM064M as confirmed EOL. Buyers should still reconfirm exact DMP-14 package availability, temperature grade, and compliance for NJM064M lots before release to production.

Series: NJM064 | Datasheet: https://www.nisshinbo-microdevices.co.jp/en/pdf/datasheet/NJM062C_NJM062CA_NJM064C_NJM064CA_E.pdf
