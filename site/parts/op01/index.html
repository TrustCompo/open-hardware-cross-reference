Target product summary

OP01

ADI marks OP01 as obsolete and suggests OP177 as the replacement on the current product page. Because the OP01 was positioned as an inverting high-speed operational amplifier, teams planning a sustainment substitution should recheck gain-bandwidth, offset, stability, supply rails, and package constraints before approving OP177 as a service replacement in a legacy analog design.
