# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be useful to engineers with different backgrounds,
vendors, regions, and levels of experience. Please keep discussions technical,
specific, and respectful.

Good participation includes:

- explaining evidence clearly
- distinguishing facts from assumptions
- correcting data without personal attacks
- being explicit about validation limits
- welcoming careful questions from engineers who are checking risk

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, abusive language, spam, deceptive
commercial promotion, or knowingly submitting misleading technical claims.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that make the project
less useful or less safe for public engineering review.
